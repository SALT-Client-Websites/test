Local Dining & Small Businesses Near Whispering Pines

One of the many benefits of living in Paulding, Ohio, is the easy access to local dining spots, cozy coffee shops, and family-friendly restaurants. Must-Visit Coffee & Café Spots

When you need your morning coffee, a midday pick-me-up, or a place to connect with friends, these local spots are worth checking out:

  • Grounded Coffee & Crumbs – A beloved local café on Perry Street serving handcrafted coffee, fresh baked goods, and light bites in a warm, welcoming space. Perfect for breakfast or a casual meet-up downtown.

  • Fuel Good Energy – A local coffee & smoothie spot with a focus on energizing drinks and light refreshments — great for an on-the-go morning.

  • Deja Brew by Cassy – A local coffee food truck serving fresh brews and seasonal offerings — ideal for grabbing a coffee on your way somewhere.

  • Past Time Cafe – While known as a family-friendly restaurant, this classic diner serves solid coffee alongside hearty breakfast and lunch options, making it a favorite local gathering place.

  • Genesis House (nearby Antwerp) – A cozy café that's just a short drive away if you feel like exploring beyond Paulding — delicious coffee and a relaxed atmosphere.

These coffee shops and cafés make it easy to enjoy your morning routine or take a break from work, all conveniently located for residents in Paulding.

Local Restaurants & Eateries Worth Trying

From classic comfort food to casual dining, Paulding's restaurant scene has several standouts:

  • Past Time Cafe – A local favorite with a home-cooked vibe, serving breakfast, lunch, and classic diner favorites. The welcoming atmosphere and strong reviews make this a go-to spot.

  • China Wok – A popular local choice for Chinese cuisine, ideal for take-out or dinner with friends and family.

  • Paulding Pancake House & Restaurant – Just a short drive from central Paulding, this restaurant delivers comfort food classics any time of day — especially pancakes and breakfast meals.
